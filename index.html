About Ignito Corporation

Ignito Corporation is a digitally driven startup founded in 2025 with a mission to bring smart, scalable, and affordable tech solutions to local businesses across India. Officially registered as a Sole Proprietorship under the MSME initiative of the Government of India, Ignito Corporation bears the MSME Registration Number: UDYAM-MP-37-0051379.

Our flagship project — the Smart LPG Distribution App — revolutionizes how gas distributors operate by enabling digital tracking, automated delivery records, analytics, reporting, workforce management, and real-time performance dashboards. Built in just one month by an 8-member team in remote collaboration, the app stands as a testament to our execution capabilities and innovation-first approach.

From startups to enterprises, we offer end-to-end services including Website Development, Mobile App Development, UI/UX Design, Backend/API setup, Hosting, SEO, Deployment & Maintenance — all under one digital roof. Our solutions are cost-effective, responsive, and tailored to client needs.

As we continue to scale, Ignito remains committed to solving real-world problems with smart technology — empowering the next generation of businesses through innovation, collaboration, and digital transformation.

Project

Smart LPG Distribution App is Ignito Corporation’s flagship project. It is designed to streamline LPG delivery, tracking, stock management, and worker-distributor coordination.

Built completely in remote mode within just one month, this project showcases the power of digital teamwork and innovation. An 8-member team worked collaboratively under expert mentorship to deliver a fully functional, scalable, and modern solution tailored specifically for the LPG industry.

With robust role-based access, automated delivery tracking, and performance dashboards, the app empowers distributors, managers, and workers to manage operations smartly across India — all in one unified platform.

Project Roadmap

Phase 1

Ideation and Research - Understanding the LPG distribution system and identifying gaps.

Phase 2

App Design & Development - Creating the Smart LPG Distribution App with roles.

Phase 3

Testing and Internal Team Trials - Ensuring tracking accuracy and efficiency.

Phase 4

Subscription Integration & Deployment - Monetization via Razorpay.

Phase 5

Team Expansion & Market Testing - Enhancing with feedback and scaling features.
