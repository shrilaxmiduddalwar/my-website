About Ignito Corporation

Ignito Corporation was established in 2025 as a sole proprietorship and is officially registered under the Indian MSME scheme with registration number UDYAM-MP-37-0051379.

Our first project — the Smart LPG Distribution App — is designed to simplify and automate the LPG delivery process by enabling digital record-keeping, tracking, and workforce management for distributors.

As a startup, our focus is on bringing digital solutions to real-world problems through smart automation and accessible platforms.

Ignito Corporation is committed to building tools that help local businesses grow and operate more efficiently while contributing to the broader goal of India's digital future.

Project Roadmap

Phase 1

Ideation and Research - Understanding the LPG distribution system and identifying gaps.

Phase 2

App Design & Development - Creating the Smart LPG Distribution App with roles.

Phase 3

Testing and Internal Team Trials - Ensuring tracking accuracy and efficiency.

Phase 4

Subscription Integration & Deployment - Monetization via Razorpay.

Phase 5

Team Expansion & Market Testing - Enhancing with feedback and scaling features.

The Smart LPG Distribution App is Ignito Corporation’s flagship project. It is designed to streamline LPG delivery, tracking, stock management, and worker-distributor coordination. Built entirely in remote mode within just one month, the project showcases the strength of digital teamwork and passion-driven innovation. Our 8-member team worked collaboratively under expert mentorship to deliver a functional, scalable, and modern solution tailored for the LPG industry. With robust role-based access and automation, the app empowers distributors, managers, and field workers across India.
